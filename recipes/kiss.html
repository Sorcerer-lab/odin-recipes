PEANUT BUTTER KISS COOKIES

These peanut butter cookies are topped with a chocolate Hershey Kiss to make classic peanut butter blossoms.

  • Sugar
  • Peanut butter
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  1. Stir the sugar, peanut butter, and egg together to make the cookie dough.
  2. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges are set.
  4. Press a Hershey's Kiss into each warm cookie, then let cool on a wire rack.
